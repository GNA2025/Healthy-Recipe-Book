Chai Puding with Fruits

  • Chia seeds are an excellent source of omega-3, fiber, and protein.
  • Almond milk and fruits add a delicious flavor along with extra vitamins and nutrients to this pudding.
  • This pudding is energy-boosting and helps improve digestion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk (any kind: dairy, almond, oat, etc.)
  • 3 chai tea bags (or 1 tbsp loose chai tea)
  • 3–4 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2–3 tbsp honey or maple syrup (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of cinnamon (optional)

For the Fruit Topping (choose your favorites):

  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ cup strawberries, sliced
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ kiwi, chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped nuts (e.g. almonds, pistachios, or walnuts)
  • A few mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Brew the Chai Milk: Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium heat. Add the chai tea bags (or loose tea in an infuser). Simmer for 5–7 minutes to infuse the flavor. Stir occasionally. Remove tea bags and let the milk cool slightly.
  2. Make the Pudding Base: In a bowl or jar, combine the chia seeds, vanilla, sweetener (honey or maple syrup), and the warm chai-infused milk. Mix well to avoid clumps.
  3. Chill: Cover the bowl or jar and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight. The chia seeds will absorb the liquid and form a pudding-like texture
  4. Serve with Fruit: Once thickened, stir the pudding to fluff it up. Top with your choice of fresh fruits, chopped nuts, and mint if desired. Optionally sprinkle some cinnamon or drizzle extra honey.

    5. Note:

    You can blend the pudding for a smoother texture. Coconut milk or cashew milk also taste great in this recipe. For extra protein, add Greek yogurt to the pudding before serving.
